WE BELIEVE IN CULTURE!

Wynwood is an up and coming neighborhood, very colorful and the fact that there’s a lot of artist expression and it was named one of the coolest hipster neighborhoods in the USA.

Exactly because it “SAN PAUL GALLERY urban art “ opened the doors in the bustling Art District in Miami on October 2014.

The gallery focused on young and already established worldwide artists of street art, creating an interchange between diverse forms of cultural expression.

The gallery focused on young and already established worldwide artists of street art, creating an interchange between diverse forms of cultural expression.

The space offers a program of temporary performances and exhibitions to promote and consolidate the artists in the American art market. The artists show their works on canvas, drawings, photos and sculptures. With intense programming the San Paul Gallery exhibitions complement each other, and also promote a lively movement in the space on event days.

ARTISTS

In 1999 BustArt began his artistic career with classic Graffiti. Until 2005, he became deeply with the whole spectrum of Graffiti and reached a new level of identification with the various facets of the subculture.

Cobo is an artist from the French town of Albi, Tarn in the Haute-Savoie. Cobo’s original painting inspiration came from the Hip Hop culture, he grew up immersed in this scene (rap, dance, graffiti and DJing).

A self-thought visual artist, EVA O, creates vibrant and playful images inspired by nature and feminine beauty.

Cesar de Almeida, born in 1974 in the city of São Paulo, is married to Verônica Soares, father of Luana and Maria Clara. He became known by the name of "Dicesarlove" for developing works that seek the noblest feelings of people.

Luiz Moreira was born in the city of São Paulo in 1989. He graduated in Social Communication at Centro Universitário Toledo in Araçatuba, interior of the state. During the period of studies at the university, his interest in photography surfaced. He then travels through the northeast of the country where he performs as his first photographs of a professional nature.

Vander has been telling stories with pictures since he was a little boy. That very affection for securing fleeting moments and the allure of his surroundings set him on many journeys during which he captured bewitching and beautiful images of the world in his landscape photos.

Involved with art from a young age under the influence of his mother, he studied Design and Advertising at Methodist University of São Paulo, as well as other courses such as Urban Art, Spray Techniques and Acrylic Paint Techniques at the ABRA institution in São Paulo.

Guilherme Matsumoto, better known as xGuix, is the grandson of Japanese immigrants, which greatly influenced his way of acting, thinking and also creating. He grew up in the Vila Monumento neighborhood, where he lived a lot with the street and its surroundings, like the Cambuci neighborhood, from which he was highly influenced by the graffiti they had there.

Intercontinental artist Solus has used spray paint as his main medium, leaving his mark in Europe, America and Asia on walls, canvas and sculpture. His successful use of colour and positive imagery evokes a sense of hope and leaves a mark that is undeniably his. Solus is based in Dublin and can often be found in New York City.

also as artist KROKIN was born in 1993 in Rostov-on-Don in South Russia. At the age of 9 he followed his mother in Naples in Italy. During teenage years he was involved in a underground scene of skateboarding, electronic music and graffiti between Naples and Rome.

Hi, I'm el xupet negre, graffiti artist since 1987, urban artist and precursor of Logoart. Since then, we have been educating the people of our neighborhoods, institutions, press, politics ... so that graffiti could be recognized as an art.

Uri, otherwise known as Uriginal, is an artist based in Barcelona, who has gained massive recognition for his art which details, a mixture of historical art pieces with modern elements. By taking the subjects from famous works of art he brings a new perspective of life to his works by adding bright colours and bold lines.

Kenor is a Barcelona artist who uses simple shapes and strong colors to create art in many different fields, such as canvas, walls, installations and even clothes. Started painting in 1990, son of a sevillan painter and photographer, emerged into street art in the late eighties and decided to share his art, first with a barrio (neighborhood) L’Hospitalet in Barcelona and that the rest of the city, and finally – with the rest of the world.

Kogaone is a French artist based in Metz. His works touch the viewer's spirit with their dark irony, varied palettes and unusual or even disturbing combinations. Attentive observer of his contemporaries and their often contradictory natures, he explores the alteration, the paradox, the embarrassment that arise when the familiar is deformed or fractured by external influences.
